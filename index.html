About

Chronsemble is an interactive spatio-temporal visualization developed at the Laboratory for Analytical Sciences on the campus of North Carolina State University. This tool is designed to enable exploration of data tagged with temporal and spatial coordinates.

Chronsemble includes modifications to the timeline example developed by Reinhard Engel
